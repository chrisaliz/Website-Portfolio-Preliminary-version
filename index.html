About me

Hello, there! Thanks for visiting my webstie!

My name is Christie, and I am an aspiring artist. This has been one of my longest dreams as a child: having the skills to draw just about anything. I enjoy drawing in my free time, with styles mainly being cartoon and chibi characters. I find cartoon and chibi characters really easy to draw since it requires less detailed aspects. Below this mini intro are some samples of my completed drawings and some work in progress, so feel free to look around.

But besides that, my other interests align with those of an adventurer! I love looking at scenic places and traveling! So far, my travels have all been domestic. You can also see that I travel with my family because they just make everything better! So if you ever travel, don't go alone!

Traveling

As I mentioned in my bio, one of my other interests is to travel! However, I have been only to China and a few states within the US, so I'm hoping that I get the chance to go out and explore the world sometime in the future!
